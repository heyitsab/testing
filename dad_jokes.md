# Dad Jokes 🤣

A collection of the finest dad jokes known to humanity.

---

1. **Why don't scientists trust atoms?**
   Because they make up everything.

2. **I'm reading a book about anti-gravity.**
   It's impossible to put down.

3. **Did you hear about the mathematician who's afraid of negative numbers?**
   He'll stop at nothing to avoid them.

4. **Why can't you give Elsa a balloon?**
   Because she'll let it go.

5. **I used to hate facial hair...**
   But then it grew on me.

6. **Why did the scarecrow win an award?**
   Because he was outstanding in his field.

7. **I'm on a seafood diet.**
   I see food and I eat it.

8. **What do you call cheese that isn't yours?**
   Nacho cheese.

9. **Why did the bicycle fall over?**
   Because it was two-tired.

10. **I told my wife she was drawing her eyebrows too high.**
    She looked surprised.

11. **What do you call a fake noodle?**
    An impasta.

12. **How do you organize a space party?**
    You planet.

13. **What's brown and sticky?**
    A stick.

14. **Why can't Elsa have a balloon?**
    Because she'll let it go. *(Yes, it's so good it's on here twice.)*

15. **I asked my dog what two minus two is.**
    He said nothing.

16. **What do you call a sleeping dinosaur?**
    A dino-snore.

17. **I used to be a banker...**
    But I lost interest.

18. **Why do cows wear bells?**
    Because their horns don't work.

19. **What do you call a bear with no teeth?**
    A gummy bear.

20. **I would tell you a joke about construction...**
    But I'm still working on it.

---

*Warning: Side effects may include eye-rolling, groaning, and uncontrollable laughter.*
