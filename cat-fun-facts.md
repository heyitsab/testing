# 🐱 Fun Facts About Cats

1. **Cats sleep a lot.** Cats sleep between 12 and 16 hours a day, making them one of the sleepiest animals on the planet.

2. **Unique nose prints.** Just like human fingerprints, every cat's nose print is unique.

3. **Purring isn't just happiness.** Cats purr not only when they're content but also when they're stressed, injured, or giving birth. Purring may help them self-soothe and even promote healing.

4. **Incredible jumpers.** Cats can jump up to six times their own body length in a single leap.

5. **Night vision.** Cats can see in light levels six times lower than what a human needs, thanks to a special reflective layer in their eyes called the *tapetum lucidum*.

6. **A group of cats is called a clowder.** A male cat is called a tom, a female is called a queen, and a young cat is called a kitten.

7. **Cats have 32 ear muscles.** This allows them to rotate their ears 180 degrees and pinpoint the source of a sound with remarkable precision.

8. **The oldest known pet cat is ~9,500 years old.** A burial site in Cyprus contains the remains of a human and a cat buried together, suggesting cats have been companions to humans for thousands of years.

9. **Cats "talk" mostly to humans.** Adult cats rarely meow at other cats — they primarily developed this vocalization to communicate with people.

10. **Righting reflex.** Cats have a flexible spine and a righting reflex that allows them to almost always land on their feet when they fall.

11. **Whiskers are sensory tools.** A cat's whiskers are roughly as wide as its body, helping it gauge whether it can fit through a space.

12. **Catnip affects big cats too.** Lions and tigers also respond to catnip, experiencing the same euphoric rolling and rubbing behavior as domestic cats.
