# 🐱 Fun Facts About Cats

1. **Cats sleep a lot.** Cats sleep between 12 and 16 hours a day, making them one of the sleepiest animals on the planet.

2. **A group of cats is called a clowder.** A group of kittens is called a kindle.

3. **Cats can't taste sweetness.** Unlike humans, cats lack the taste receptors needed to detect sweet flavors.

4. **Each cat's nose print is unique.** Just like human fingerprints, no two cats have the same nose print pattern.

5. **Cats have a third eyelid.** Called the "nictitating membrane," it helps protect and moisten the eye.

6. **A cat's purr can heal bones.** The frequency of a cat's purr (25–50 Hz) has been shown to promote bone density and healing.

7. **Cats walk like camels and giraffes.** They move both right legs, then both left legs — a gait shared by only a few animals.

8. **House cats share 95.6% of their DNA with tigers.** They also share many behaviors, like scent marking and pouncing.

9. **Cats can rotate their ears 180 degrees.** This helps them pinpoint the exact source of a sound.

10. **The oldest known pet cat is 9,500 years old.** Evidence of a cat buried with its human was found in a Neolithic grave in Cyprus.

11. **Cats can make over 100 different sounds.** Dogs can only make about 10.

12. **Cats spend about 30–50% of their waking hours grooming.** This keeps them clean and regulates their body temperature.

13. **A cat's whiskers are roughly as wide as its body.** They use them to judge whether they can fit through a gap.

14. **Cats always land on their feet.** This is thanks to a reflex called the "righting reflex," which allows them to orient themselves mid-fall.

15. **Isaac Newton is credited with inventing the cat door.** He reportedly cut a hole in his study door so his cat wouldn't interrupt his experiments.
