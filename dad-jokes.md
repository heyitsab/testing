# 😂 Dad Jokes: Extra Funny Edition

> ⚠️ Warning: These jokes are so bad, they're good. Side effects may include eye-rolling 🙄, groaning 😩, and uncontrollable laughter 🤣.

---

1. 📚 **I'm reading a book about anti-gravity.**
   *It's impossible to put down.*

2. 😲 **I told my wife she was drawing her eyebrows too high.**
   *She looked surprised.*

3. ⚛️ **Why don't scientists trust atoms?**
   *Because they make up everything.*

4. 🧔 **I used to hate facial hair...**
   *But then it grew on me.*

5. 🐶 **I asked my dog what two minus two is.**
   *He said nothing.*

6. 🏆 **Why did the scarecrow win an award?**
   *Because he was outstanding in his field.*

7. 🍤 **I'm on a seafood diet.**
   *I see food and I eat it.*

8. 🍬 **Did you hear about the guy who invented Lifesavers?**
   *He made a mint.*

9. 🎈 **Why can't you give Elsa a balloon?**
   *Because she'll let it go.*

10. 🏗️ **I would tell you a construction joke...**
    *But I'm still working on it.*

11. 🧀 **What do you call cheese that isn't yours?**
    *Nacho cheese.*

12. 🔤 **I only know 25 letters of the alphabet.**
    *I don't know y.*

13. 🍝 **What do you call a fake noodle?**
    *An impasta.*

14. 🚲 **Why did the bicycle fall over?**
    *Because it was two-tired.*

15. 🐱 **I asked my cat if she was hungry.**
    *She said, "Meow." I said, "I didn't ask for your opinion."*

16. 🐻 **What do you call a bear with no teeth?**
    *A gummy bear.*

17. 📅 **I'm afraid for the calendar.**
    *Its days are numbered.*

18. 📖 **Why did the math book look so stressed?**
    *Because it had too many problems.*

19. 📄 **I tried to write a joke about paper...**
    *Tear-able.*

20. 🐟 **What do you call a fish without eyes?**
    *A fsh.*

21. 😴 **Did you hear about the kidnapping at school?**
    *It's fine — he woke up.*

22. 🐄 **Why do cows wear bells?**
    *Because their horns don't work.*

23. 🔋 **I gave all my dead batteries away today.**
    *Free of charge.*

24. 🪵 **What's brown and sticky?**
    *A stick.*

25. 💪 **I told my doctor I broke my arm in two places.**
    *He told me to stop going to those places.*
