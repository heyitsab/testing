# Dad Jokes

1. Why don't scientists trust atoms? Because they make up everything!

2. I'm reading a book about anti-gravity. It's impossible to put down.

3. Did you hear about the mathematician who's afraid of negative numbers? He'll stop at nothing to avoid them.

4. Why did the scarecrow win an award? Because he was outstanding in his field.

5. I used to hate facial hair, but then it grew on me.

6. What do you call cheese that isn't yours? Nacho cheese!

7. Why can't you give Elsa a balloon? Because she'll let it go.

8. I would tell you a construction pun, but I'm still working on it.

9. What do you call a fish without eyes? A fsh.

10. Why did the bicycle fall over? Because it was two-tired!
