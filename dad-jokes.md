# Dad Jokes 🤣

1. Why don't scientists trust atoms? **Because they make up everything.**

2. I'm reading a book about anti-gravity. **It's impossible to put down.**

3. Did you hear about the guy who invented Lifesavers? **He made a mint.**

4. Why can't you give Elsa a balloon? **Because she'll let it go.**

5. I used to hate facial hair, but then it grew on me.

6. Why did the scarecrow win an award? **Because he was outstanding in his field.**

7. I'm on a seafood diet. **I see food and I eat it.**

8. What do you call a fake noodle? **An impasta.**

9. Why did the bicycle fall over? **Because it was two-tired.**

10. Did you hear about the cheese factory that exploded? **There was nothing left but de-brie.**

11. I asked my dog what two minus two is. **He said nothing.**

12. Why do cows wear bells? **Because their horns don't work.**

13. What do you call a bear with no teeth? **A gummy bear.**

14. I only know 25 letters of the alphabet. **I don't know y.**

15. What do you call cheese that isn't yours? **Nacho cheese.**

16. How do you organize a space party? **You planet.**

17. I would tell you a joke about construction, but I'm still working on it.

18. What do you call a sleeping dinosaur? **A dino-snore.**

19. Why can't Elsa have a balloon? **Because she'll let it go.** *(She never learns.)*

20. What's brown and sticky? **A stick.**
